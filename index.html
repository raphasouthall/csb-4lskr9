Juliet Timms, Founder and CEO

Leadership and Advisory Consultancy

Juliet champions and supports leaders to consistently be the best they can be. Acting as a catalyst for transformational moments along the leadership journey, she helps raise leaders to a level of brilliance perhaps even when they didn't realise it was possible

The combination of her 20 years as an agency practitioner, 20 years as a C-Suite level headhunter and her formal business coaching training gives her a unique ability to work alongside leaders, removing obstacles, unlocking ideas and helping define the way a leader wants to lead

Expertise

  • Leadership development

  • Executive and leadership team coaching

  • Career development and coaching
Juliet started her career at one of the most admired creative agencies in the world, BBH. She set up BBH's integrated agency in 1987 which she led for the next 14 years before moving into a growth role across the full agency group. In a high performance environment, she learnt about leadership and what it takes to be outstanding. It was this experience that prompted her move into headhunting. She joined Kendall Tarrant in 2003 where she challenged and inspired leaders from all over the world in the media, marketing and communications industries.
In 2007, she set up her own headhunting business with two partners. Grace Blue was founded on the belief that finding the right talent and supporting that talent was the most important factor positively impacting the success of any business. Grace Blue has grown to be a global organisation, respected all over the world for finding and developing transformational leadership talent.
Two decades later, she has now trained as a business coach with one of the most respected and exclusive business coaching programmes in the world - Meyler Campbell. This intensive training has enhanced her coaching skills and the knowledge of how to deliver the greatest impact. A member of Harvard's Institute of Coaching, she is part of a community of professional coaches who are constantly learning and sharing their knowledge and experience. Having stepped aside from her CEO role within Grace Blue, Juliet has now moved onto Grace Blue's global advisory board, leading their business coaching practice. Her experience of working with C-level executives provides her with a unique foundation to develop her clients effectively within their organisation environment.
